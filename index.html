Welcome to the Frontier

We are an independent research institute dedicated to revisiting fundamental assumptions in physics, particularly concerning dark phenomena, gravitational interactions, and the potential for macroscopic quantum effects in celestial systems. Our work bridges rigorous theoretical inquiry with novel data-driven approaches.

Our current focus includes the Dynamics-Gravity-Centrifugal Force materialization model, tests of chiral-scale decoupling, and high-precision datasets for three-body gravitational dynamics.

Research Focus

Dynamic Gravity & Dark Phenomena

Reconciling observations with modified gravity by introducing dynamic gravitational components and re-examining the nature of centrifugal force, offering alternative explanations for dark matter and dark energy.

Macroscopic Quantum Signatures

Investigating potential quantum-gravitational effects at planetary scales, including quantized orbital angular momentum candidates and chiral-scale decoupling mechanisms in gravitational systems.

High-Precision Celestial Datasets

Creating and curating benchmark dynamical datasets (e.g., Sun-Earth-Moon three-body) for testing classical and alternative gravitational theories, ensuring reproducibility and open science.
